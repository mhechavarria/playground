The Incredible Hulk

This character is known for his uncontrollable rage and unpredictable nature. The angrier the Hulk becomes, the stronger he becomes, making him one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel universe. Despite his immense power, the Hulk often struggles to control his temper, leading to destructive rampages.
