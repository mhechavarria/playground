The Incredible Hulk

The Hulk, also known as Dr. Bruce Banner, is a superhuman with green skin and immense strength. Standing at around seven feet tall, he is nearly invulnerable and possesses raw power that increases with his anger. Despite his size, he is surprisingly agile and can leap great distances. The Hulk is a formidable force in combat, capable of toppling buildings and overpowering any adversary. His alter ego, Dr. Banner, retains his intelligence, allowing for strategic thinking amidst chaos. With a distinctive roar, the Hulk strikes fear into his enemies as he unleashes his overwhelming power.
